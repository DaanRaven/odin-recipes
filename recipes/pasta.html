Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty Pasta has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This Pasta recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Pasta noodles: Use store-bought or homemade Pasta noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this Pasta extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the Pasta when you cut into it.
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the Pasta according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the Pasta rest before serving.
